# Loyiha maqsadi

Ushbu loyiha “dasturchilar tomonidan dasturchilar uchun” modeli sifatida qurilgan.
Mamlakatimizda dasturlashni o‘rgatadigan markazlar ko‘paygani sari amaliy mashqlarni, daraja oshirish,
“oddiy “CRUD” dan tashqari nima qilish mumkin?” kabi muammolarni qoniqtirish maqsadida [dowhile.uz](/) loyihasini yo‘lga qo‘ydik.


Loyiha dasturchilar uchun turli xil [mavzularni](/topics) o‘rganish, yoki bilimlarni mustahkamlash imkoniyatini beradi.
Bundan tashqari siz mavzuga oid o‘z darsliklaringizni [yaratishingiz](/editor) ham mumkin. 


Loyiha [leetcode.com](https://leetcode.com), [codecrafters.io](https://codecrafters.io/), [exercism.org](https://exercism.org/),
[kodekloud.com](https://kodekloud.com/) va [hackthebox.com](https://www.hackthebox.com/) kabi platformalardan ilhomlangan.
